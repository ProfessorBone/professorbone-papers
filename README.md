# Professor Bone Lab — Papers

Research on governed agentic AI systems: constitutional runtime architectures,
epistemic governance, and the engineering of constitutionally bounded agents.

**Author:** Clarence "Faheem" Downs (Professor Bone Lab)
**Site:** [professorbone.com](https://professorbone.com)

## Lines of Work

### Constitutional Runtime Computation (CRC)

The primary research line. Formalizes a constitutional runtime architecture for
agentic systems in which governance defines the topology of reachable state
transitions rather than operating as post-hoc evaluation.

The series has three layers: a core paper, formal companion papers that mature
the architecture by closing identified gaps, and domain application documents
that demonstrate the architecture instantiated in real systems.

[Go to the CRC series](crc/README.md)

---

### Epistemic Governance

A cluster of papers on how agents know what they know, where knowledge fails to
become operative, and how orchestrators can become epistemically captured by the
systems they govern.

[Go to the Epistemic Governance cluster](epistemic-governance/README.md)
